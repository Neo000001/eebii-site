Train The Weakest Link

Studies show that 90% of massive corporate data breaches result not from sophisticated software hacks, but through simple employee manipulation—clicking a bad email link. An expensive firewall is useless if internal staff blindly opens the door for hackers. Eebii prepares your staff to act defensively on the internet while increasing their daily technological fluency.

What We Handle For You
