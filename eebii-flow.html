Platform Features

Everything Your Business Needs

79+ features built and ready. From single message sends to bulk campaigns hitting 10,000+ contacts.

Bulk Messaging Engine

Send personalized, template-approved messages to thousands of contacts simultaneously via the official WhatsApp Cloud API. No bans, no grey-area tools.

Campaign Automation

Schedule drip campaigns, follow-up sequences, and reminder flows to go out automatically. Pause, resume, and track every campaign in real-time.

Transparent Wallet Billing

Prepaid wallet system with full cost breakdown: Meta Cost + Platform Fee + Tax. No hidden charges. Auto-recharge when balance runs low.

Real-Time Analytics

Delivery receipts, read rates, click tracking and campaign performance dashboards. Know exactly what is working every single minute.

Real-Time Inbox

Threaded conversation management, agent assignment, internal notes, and WebSocket live updates. Your team can handle thousands of customer chats.

Chatbot Auto-Replies

Build keyword-triggered instant responses. Customers get immediate answers around the clock without human agents being present.

Team & Role Management

Multi-tenant workspaces with full RBAC. Roles: Owner, Manager, Agent. Give your accountant read-only, your sales reps send-only.

Contact Management

Import via CSV, segment by tags, detect duplicates, validate international numbers. Full contact history with every conversation.

Enterprise Security

Dual-token JWT auth, bcrypt hashing, account lockout, rate limiting, HMAC webhook signatures, full Audit Log for compliance.

Ad & Campaign Manager

Create and manage WhatsApp Click-to-Message ad campaigns. Track ad performance, leads, and cost-per-acquisition directly from the dashboard.

50+ REST API Endpoints

Full programmatic access. Integrate Eebii Flow with your Shopify store, CRM, or Zapier workflow. Webhooks, API keys and complete documentation.
