Privacy Policy

Updated | March 2026 | Category: Official

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Eebii, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information across our entire ecosystem, including our free browser-based tools, Eebii Flow communication suite, and Eebii Academy training programs.

1. Information Collection

We collect information in three ways to provide better services to all our users:

  • Information you give us: This includes your name, email address, and business details provided when you contact us for support or enroll in Eebii Academy.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our services, such as the tools you access and the frequency of your interactions.
  • Local Data Storage: For tools like the GST Invoice Generator and PDF Suite, your data is processed locally in your browser and is NEVER uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly opt-in for Cloud Sync.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect, and improve them. Specifically:

  • To process your service requests and support tickets.
  • To manage your enrollment and progress in Eebii Academy courses.
  • To ensure the security and reliability of our IT infrastructure.
  • To communicate with you about updates to our tools or ecosystem.

3. Data Sharing and Transparency

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, and individuals outside of Eebii unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of Eebii when we have your consent to do so.
  • External processing: We provide personal information to our trusted affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.

4. Information Security

We work hard to protect Eebii and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and maintain strict physical and digital access controls.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use various technologies to collect and store information when you visit an Eebii service, and this may include using cookies or similar technologies to identify your browser or device. We use these technologies to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services.

6. Contacting Us Regarding Privacy

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Privacy Team at contact@eebii.com.
