How to Create a GST Invoice Online in India (Complete 2026 Guide)

Issuing a GST-compliant invoice is mandatory for businesses registered under Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India. Whether you are a freelancer, consultant, startup, or small business, proper invoicing ensures legal compliance and professional credibility.

What Is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a legal document issued by a GST-registered seller to a buyer. It contains transaction details, GST breakdown, and total payable amount.

Mandatory Details in a GST Invoice

  • Seller name, address & GSTIN
  • Buyer name, address & GSTIN (if registered)
  • Invoice number & date
  • HSN / SAC code (if applicable)
  • Taxable value
  • CGST, SGST or IGST breakup
  • Grand total payable

Difference Between CGST, SGST & IGST

CGST & SGST (Same State)

If seller and buyer are in the same state, GST is split equally into CGST and SGST. Example: 18% GST ? 9% CGST + 9% SGST.

IGST (Different States)

If seller and buyer are in different states, full GST is charged as IGST. Example: 18% GST ? 18% IGST.
