Eebii Flow: WhatsApp Bulk Messaging Revolution for Indian SMEs

By Eebii Team | December 2025 | Category: Tools

WhatsApp is not just a messaging app for Indian consumers. With over 500 million active users in India alone, it is the single most powerful direct communication channel available to any business today. And yet, most Indian SMEs are still using personal WhatsApp accounts to send bulk messages manually — one contact at a time.

That changes with Eebii Flow.

What is Eebii Flow

Eebii Flow is our upcoming WhatsApp Business API platform. It is currently in active production and will launch in 2026 as a dedicated SaaS tool for Indian businesses to send bulk messages, automate campaign sequences, and manage thousands of customer conversations from a single clean dashboard — without ever risking a WhatsApp account ban.

Why WhatsApp Marketing is Massively Underutilized

Email marketing in India has an average open rate of roughly 18%. SMS marketing fares slightly better. But WhatsApp message open rates in India average an astonishing 98%. When someone receives a message on WhatsApp, they open it. Almost always.

Imagine sending a product launch announcement, a sale reminder, or an invoice reminder and knowing that 98 out of every 100 recipients read it within minutes. That is the power Eebii Flow puts directly into your hands.

What Eebii Flow Will Offer

Bulk Messaging: Send personalized, template-approved messages to your entire customer list simultaneously.

Campaign Automation: Set up drip campaigns that automatically send follow-ups on day 1, day 3, and day 7 after a product inquiry.

Chatbot Auto-Reply: Build keyword-triggered instant replies so customers get immediate responses around the clock.

Analytics Dashboard: Real-time delivery receipts, read rates, and click tracking to measure every campaign's exact performance.
