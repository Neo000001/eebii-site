To fundamentally comprehend the necessity of Eebii Flow, one must strictly look at the brutal analytics of modern marketing. When a consumer’s phone vibrates with a distinct WhatsApp notification, human psychological conditioning practically guarantees they will open it to clear the notification badge, usually within three minutes of delivery.
