Announcing Eebii Academy: IT Training Meets AI Game Design

By Eebii Team | March 2026 | Academy News

The gap between standard corporate tooling and the absolute cutting-edge of Artificial Intelligence is closing rapidly. At Eebii, we realize that building world-class corporate infrastructure is only half the battle. The other half is ensuring the workforce utilizing that infrastructure is empowered, educated, and continuously upskilling.

That is why we are incredibly excited to announce the upcoming launch of Eebii Academy—a premier, dual-track e-learning platform launching later in 2026.

Why Two Tracks?

When engineering Eebii Academy, we realized we catered to two distinct profiles. First, our traditional small business clients who need practical, immediate IT upskilling. Second, the creative professionals and studio contractors actively looking to integrate generative AI into their 3D pipelines.

We built Academy to accommodate both seamlessly.

Track 1: Corporate IT & Productivity

This track is engineered specifically for office staff, HR managers, and corporate teams. It strips out the heavy technical jargon and teaches practical computing.

  • Cyber Defense: Recognizing hyper-realistic phishing emails, understanding local encryption, and securing remote VPN access.
  • Software Mastery: Deep-dives into maximizing CRMs, Eebii PDF Suite, and Microsoft Office environments.
  • Level-1 Support: Teaching department managers how to execute basic troubleshooting (LAN checks, printer resets) to radically reduce external IT support costs.

Track 2: Creative AI & Game Design

For the creators. This high-end track merges artistic vision with the raw computational power of modern AI tools.

  • Game Design in Unreal Engine: Utilizing AI from the storyboarding and visual reference phases, all the way into blueprinting and optimization.
  • Blender with AI: Accelerating the 3D pipeline by letting AI assist with complex modeling, substance painter texturing, and final rendering overlays.
  • VFX Masterclass: Industry-standard compositing through Adobe After Effects and Davinci Resolve to bring 3D assets to life.

How to Get Involved

Registrations and curriculums are currently being finalized. Our expert instructors are recording the highest quality screenshares, developing assignments, and integrating AI copilot workflows directly into the course framework.

You can reserve your spot in the initial launch cohort by joining our master waitlist right now. You can explicitly select whether you are interested in the Professional Corporate IT track or the Creative Game Design track directly on the form.
