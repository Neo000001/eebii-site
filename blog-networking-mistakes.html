📡 Top 5 Office Networking Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

By Eebii IT Solutions • July 2025 • Category: Network Setup

Setting up an office network sounds simple — until things stop working. Many small businesses unknowingly make critical mistakes in their networking that lead to slow internet, security holes, and constant frustration.

Here are the top 5 networking mistakes we see (and how you can avoid them).

1. ❌ Using the Default Router Settings

Leaving the default password, Wi-Fi name (SSID), or admin access open is a major security risk.

Fix: Change all default credentials and disable WPS. Set a strong Wi-Fi password.

2. 🧱 Putting the Router in a Bad Spot

Hiding the router under desks or in closets reduces signal strength.

Fix: Place the router in a central open area, away from metal objects and walls.

3. 🔌 Mixing Too Many Devices on Wi-Fi

Smart TVs, phones, laptops, printers — all sharing Wi-Fi? That causes drops.

Fix: Use LAN (wired) connections for static devices (like desktops & printers).

4. 🔒 No Firewall or Guest Network

Allowing guests or staff to use your primary Wi-Fi exposes sensitive data.

Fix: Set up a guest Wi-Fi network and enable a proper firewall (like FortiGate or SonicWall).

5. 💽 Not Backing Up Router Configuration

After a power failure or reset, all settings vanish — unless you saved them.

Fix: Backup your router config after setup and store it safely.

✅ Conclusion

Your network is your business's backbone. A few smart decisions can save you hours of downtime and protect your data.
